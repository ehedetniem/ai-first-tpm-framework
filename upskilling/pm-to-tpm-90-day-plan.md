# PM to TPM Upskilling Plan (90 Days)

## Goal

Move from manual status reporting to AI-enabled TPM execution with consistent artifacts and stronger decision quality.

## Days 1-30: Foundations

- Adopt standard templates.
- Start weekly status cadence.
- Capture major decisions using ADRs.
- Introduce AI-assisted first drafts for updates.

## Days 31-60: Execution quality

- Improve signal quality in status updates.
- Enforce review checklist for AI outputs.
- Track recurring risks and dependency patterns.
- Shift time from formatting to problem-solving.

## Days 61-90: Operating model scale

- Standardize portfolio-level rollup.
- Normalize branch-based collaboration workflow.
- Codify governance boundaries and approval gates.
- Measure cycle time and artifact quality improvements.

## Evidence of successful transition

- Consistent weekly artifacts across programs
- Decision context preserved, not lost in threads
- Reduced manual synthesis burden
- Increased TPM time spent on judgment and alignment
