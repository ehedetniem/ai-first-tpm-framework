# AI Enablement Maturity Model

## Level 0 - Manual

- Status and decisions managed via slides and email.
- Inconsistent formatting and weak traceability.

## Level 1 - Structured

- Standard templates adopted.
- Program lifecycle and weekly cadence defined.

## Level 2 - AI Assisted

- AI drafts first-pass status and summaries.
- Humans review and approve before publishing.

## Level 3 - Orchestrated

- Artifact generation follows repeatable workflows.
- Portfolio rollups become predictable and consistent.

## Level 4 - Governed Ambient

- AI support is continuous and context-aware.
- Decision and approval gates remain human-owned.
- Learning loop improves quality over time.
