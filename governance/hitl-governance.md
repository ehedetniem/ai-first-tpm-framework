# Human-in-the-Loop Governance

This framework is AI-enabled, not AI-autonomous.

## Principle

- AI drafts and synthesizes.
- Humans approve and are accountable.

## Human approval required

- Strategic scope changes
- Budget or headcount decisions
- External partner commitments
- Sev1 escalations
- Executive-level communications

## Review checklist for AI-generated outputs

Before sending any artifact:
- Verify claims are evidence-based.
- Verify dates, owners, and dependencies.
- Verify decision status is accurate.
- Verify risks are actionable and current.
- Remove unsupported conclusions.

## Data and source boundaries

- Do not include confidential data in public artifacts.
- Maintain source traceability for critical claims.
- Label unknowns explicitly.
